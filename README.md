# HR-Analysis-Report-Power-BI-Dashboard
This Power BI report provides a comprehensive analysis of key HR metrics, focusing on attrition, workforce distribution, employee demographics, and job satisfaction insights. The dashboard enables HR teams and leadership to monitor trends, understand workforce dynamics, and support strategic decision‑making.
The HR Analysis Report includes the following major KPIs and visual insights:
1. Key HR Metrics (KPI Cards)

Employee Count: 1470
Attrition: 237
Attrition Rate: 16.12%
Active Employees: 1233
Average Age: 37

These indicators provide a high-level view of workforce health and stability.

🔍 Detailed Visual Insights
2. Department-wise Attrition (Pie Chart)
A color-coded pie chart showing attrition distribution across departments.
This helps identify which departments are experiencing the highest turnover.

3. Number of Employees by Age Group
A stacked bar chart showing employee distribution by:

Age bands (Below 25, 25–34, 35–44, 45–54, Over 55)
Gender (Male & Female)

This highlights demographic patterns and age diversity within the workforce.

4. Job Satisfaction Rating (Matrix Table)
A detailed matrix showing satisfaction ratings (1–5) across multiple job roles such as:

Sales Representative
Research Scientist
Laboratory Technician
Healthcare Representative
Manufacturing Director
…and more.

This visual helps understand employee sentiment across functions and job categories.

5. No. of Employees by Age Group (Grouped Bar Chart – Bottom Left)
Another age‑band visualization broken down by gender, helping compare male–female distribution across different age segments.

🎯 Purpose of the Dashboard
This report supports:

Tracking and reducing attrition
Understanding demographic makeup of the workforce
Monitoring employee satisfaction
Identifying risk areas across departments
Enhancing data-driven HR decision-making
